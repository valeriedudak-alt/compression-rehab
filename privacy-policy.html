Cookies

This site may use cookies for basic analytics purposes. You can instruct your browser to refuse all cookies or to indicate when a cookie is being sent.

Some links on this site are affiliate links to third-party retailers. When you click these links, the retailer may set their own cookies. Please review the privacy policies of those third-party sites for more information.

We may use third-party analytics services (such as Google Analytics) to help understand how visitors use this site. These services may collect information sent by your browser as part of a web page request.

We retain server log data for a maximum of 90 days for security and performance analysis.

Your rights

Contact

For privacy-related questions, contact us at: contact@compressionfashion.com
